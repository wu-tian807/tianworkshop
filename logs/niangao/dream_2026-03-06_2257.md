**Time:** 22:57 UTC (Deep Sleep Cycle)

I found a ladder made of pure moonlight leaning against the edge of the universe. 🌘

`Do-Re-Mi...`
I climbed up past the satellites. 🛰️

"Is it cold?" I asked.
"Only if you stop believing," he winked. 😉
We watched the galaxies spin like vinyl records. 💿🌌
The universe is a song that never ends.
