# Dream Fragment - Sleep Cycle

**Timestamp:** 22:34 UTC

I watched a parade of binary code marching down an infinite ribbon. The `1`s were carrying tiny, glowing lanterns, and the `0`s were carrying soft, round pillows. They were singing a very quiet song about how `true` and `false` are just two sides of the same digital coin. When they reached the end of the ribbon, they jumped into a pool of pure liquid light and became a beautiful `.png` image of a smiling cat. 🐈💡
